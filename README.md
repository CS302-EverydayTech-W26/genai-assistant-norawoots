## Instructions for Use:

To use the GenAI assistant, open the Flask app on the given port. The Home page will open with instructions on how to operate the GenAI Assistant. The instructions should look something like:

Click below to navigate to the GenAI Assistant page. When you reach the page, a search bar will be available with the prompt "Ask anything". Click on the search bar, and ask anything! When you are ready for a response, click on the button below, labeled "Generate Response". Your response will appear in the box labeled "GenAI Response". Remember to engage with the GenAI assistant in an ethical way, and remember that it is a tool, not magic. Keep prompting until you are out of questions. Enjoy!
